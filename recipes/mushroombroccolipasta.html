Creamy Broccoli and Mushroom pasta


Back to homepage

Description

This is a recipe I always ate growing up. It's always felt very homey and is great for a cold winter day or if you aren't feel great. It's super easy and quick to make, with very few ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1 onion
  • 2-4 cloves of garlic
  • 200-300g of tortellinis or pasta of your choice
  • 1 whole broccoli
  • 400-500g of button mushrooms
  • 1 box of boursin
  • 150ml of creme fraiche (or more to taste, you can also replace this with haver fraiche)

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil and crush garlic in a large frying pan at medium heat until slightly yellow and aromatic.
  2. Chop onions and add to garlic and stir them until they turn slightly yellow and start to smell sweet.
  3. Boil water in a water cooker or boil water in a pot until it bubbles/boils.
  4. Chop mushrooms and add to frying pan once the onions are starting to cook.
  5. Add tortellinis to boiling water and boil for 15 minutes. If you have a different pasta, you might need to time it differently and start this step later if it has a shorter cooking time.
  6. Chop or break the broccoli into small pieces and add to frying pan.
  7. Stir fry the broccoli, mushrooms, onions and garlic until everything is well done.
  8. Turn the fire down to low heat, add the creme fraiche and boursin and mix well so all the vegetables are covered.
  9. Once the pasta is done cooking, pour water out and add the pasta to the vegetables and sauce in the frying pan. Mix well until all the pasta is mixed.
  10. You are now ready to serve a delicious and warm pasta! You can also choose to serve this with some vegetarian chicken, bacon or add some smoked salmon.
