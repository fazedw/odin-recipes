Creamy Broccoli and Mushroom pasta

Description

This is a recipe I always ate growing up. It's always felt very homey and is great for a cold winter day or if you aren't feel great. It's super easy and quick to make, with very few ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1 onion
  • 2-4 cloves of garlic
  • 200-300g of tortellinis or pasta of your choice
  • 1 whole broccoli
  • 400-500g of button mushrooms
  • 1 box of boursin
  • 150ml of creme fraiche (or more to taste, you can also replace this with haver fraiche)
